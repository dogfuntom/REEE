At REEE our goal is to create recommendations for online video sharing social media content independent of online video sharing social media platform itself.  In order to be able to achieve this we collect & process necessary data [including online video viewing history, likes & dislikes and engagement] and track User’s behaviour on video sharing social media website. We collect and process necessary personal data [online video viewing history, likes & dislike, engagement] of REEE users in order to be able to provide them with the most relevant recommendation for online videos. The purpose of REEE is to allow users to  receive online video recommendations that are independent of online video sharing social media platform algorithms. This privacy policy (“Privacy Policy”) is designed to provide users with understanding of the protection of their privacy and processing of their personal data carried out by REEE and its developers in accordance with the Data Protection Act 2018. Privacy Policy provides information on the reasons for processing of User’s data, scope, protection, storage periods and sources of data, and, most importantly, User’s rights during the acquisition and processing of data.

  1. REEE collects and processes Non-Personal data of the User after installing the REEE browser extension in the form of online video viewing history. User’s Personal data may be collected and processed when the User contacts REEE directly or reports an issue or suggestion to us or other Users (via User forum) regarding the use of REEE.
  2. When the User installs REEE, we assign a unique identification number (“ID number”) to each installation for us to be able to track the amount of active REEE Users, however, the User cannot be directly or indirectly identified by the given ID number. The ID number only serves as means of separating one User’s account from another without being able to identify the User himself/herself.
